== Description ==

blueboxed is a versatile and user-friendly WordPress theme designed for bloggers and content publishers. With its clean and modern design, blueboxed offers a seamless and visually appealing experience for both content creators and readers.

blueboxed focuses on showcasing your content prominently. It offers various layout options, allowing you to present your articles, images, and multimedia in a visually appealing and organized manner. The theme's responsive design ensures that your website looks great and functions flawlessly on different devices and screen sizes.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
